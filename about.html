ABOUT US

Stave & Hoop isn't just a liquor store—it's a Eau Claire institution. As Water Street's original purveyor of fine spirits, we've been the go-to destination for locals and visitors alike since the 80s! Our shelves are stocked with an unrivaled selection of craft beers, exquisite wines, and top-shelf spirits, ensuring you'll find the perfect drink for any occasion.

But we're more than just your average bottle shop. We're the epicenter of Eau Claire's "boozegold" scene, curating a unique collection of vapes, zyns, and THC products for those seeking a different kind of buzz. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or a curious newcomer, our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to guide you through our extensive inventory and help you discover your new favorite libation.

As a proud partner of The Pickle, The Pioneer Tavern, and The Club Country Bar, we're deeply connected to the vibrant nightlife and social scene that makes Eau Claire so special. We believe that great drinks and good company go hand in hand, and we're committed to providing the highest quality products and service to enhance your experience, whether you're enjoying a night out on the town or relaxing at home.

So come on down to Stave & Hoop and discover why we're more than just a store – we're a community hub where friends gather, memories are made, and the spirit of Eau Claire comes to life.
